Momos

Ingredients:

  • 450 gm flour.
  • 1 tablespoon refined oil.
  • 2 large onion
  • 5 green chilli
  • salt as required.
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 2 teaspoon soy souce
  • water as required
  • 1 tablespoon chilli-garlic paste
  • 1/2 red bell paper

Steps To Cook:

  1. Take 1 cup all-purpose flour, ¼ teaspoon salt and ½ teaspoon oil in a bowl. Mix well with a spoon.
  2. Add 2 to 3 tablespoon water in parts and begin to knead to a firm dough. Do not make the dough soft as then it becomes difficult to shape the dumplings.
  3. Cover the dough with a moist napkin or kitchen towel. Let the dough rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Finely chop the vegetables. You will need 1.5 to 1.75 cups of finely chopped veggies.
  5. Heat oil in a thick bottomed frying pan or wok. Add ½ teaspoon of finely chopped garlic
  6. Add spring onion whites (2 small spring-onions which have been chopped finely).
  7. Add all the finely chopped vegetables.
  8. Switch off the heat and add 1 to 2 tbsp of the spring onions greens.
  9. Apply water with your finger tip or with a small pastry brush towards the circumference of doughball.
  10. .Place 2 to 3 teaspoons of vegetable stuffing in the center and steam it.
