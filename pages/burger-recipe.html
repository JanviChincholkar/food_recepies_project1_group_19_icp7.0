*INGREDIENTS*
⦿flour 80-100 gm.....
⦿red chilli powder-1tsp....
⦿green chilli paste....
⦿black papper-1tsp....
⦿salt-as per require...
⦿lemon juice-2-3 tbsp....
⦿water as require....
⦿bread crumbs-30-40 gm....
⦿butter 2-3 tbsp...
⦿bun-3pcs....
⦿tomato sauce-3-4 tbps...
⦿mayonnaise-1 tbsp....
⦿peri-peri masala....
⦿cabbage leaves...

1.Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate..
2.Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.
3.Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties. 4.Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
5.An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
Serve hota nd enjoy!
