*INGREDIENTS*
⦿ Olive oil
⦿ Boneless skinless chicken breasts
⦿ Salt
⦿ Pepper
⦿ White wine or chicken broth
⦿ Butter
⦿ Garlic
⦿ Fresh parsley, optional

1..To make this quick and easy chicken with garlic sauce, simply pound the chicken to an even thickness and then heat up a skillet!
3.2.To a large skillet, add olive oil, chicken breasts seasoned with 21 Salute Seasoning, salt, pepper, and sear on the first side for about 5 minutes.
4.Flip chicken oven and cook on the second side for about 5 minutes.
5.When the chicken is done and cooked through, remove it from the pan and allow it to rest on a plate.
6.Deglaze your pan with a splash of wine, add the butter, garlic, and cook for one minute, or until the butter has melted and the garlic is fragrant.
7.Return the chicken to the pan, toss it in the garlic butter sauce, garnish with parsley, and serve.
