Hot And Sour soup

About

Hot and sour soup is rich in manganese which is great for our overall health. This nutrient helps in bone development. Also, it helps in boosting the metabolic rate of the body




Ingredients:

  • i½ tablespoons Corn flour (corn starch) dissolved in ½ cup of water
  • ½ tablespoon Sesame oil
  • ¼ cup Red onion chopped finely
  • Ginger chopped finely
  • Garlic chopped finely
  • 1 tablespoon Celery chopped finely
  • 2 tablespoons Green beans chopped finely

Steps To Cook:

  1. First prep all the ingredients, chop the veggies (ginger, garlic, celery, onion, carrot, mushrooms, green pepper, cabbage and green beans) and keep it aside.
  2. Also dissolve the cornstarch into ½ cup of water.
  3. chopping vegetables and making cornflour slurry
  4. Now heat the oil in a saucepan on medium heat. Once hot add chopped ginger, garlic, celery and onion.
  5. Mix well and saute till onions become soft and translucent.
  6. Then add rest of the veggies.
  7. Mix well and saute for just 1 minutes. As we have chopped them pretty small they will get cooked fast.
  8. Add cooked rice and salt.
  9. Toss the rice gently so rice grains don’t break and stir fry for 2-3 minutes by tossing and mixing so all the sauce and veggies, rice are mixed properly. Lastly, garnish with spring onion greens and serve.
  10. Add water or vegetable stock.
  11. Bring it to a simmer.
  12. Add soya sauce.
  13. Also add red chili sauce
  14. Mix well. Now first stir the corn flour+water mixture as flour might settled down at the bottom of the bowl. Then pour it into the soup.
  15. Stir well.
  16. And soup is ready to serve.

Serve your dish!!
