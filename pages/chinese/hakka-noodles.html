Veg Hakka Noodles

About

Hakka noodles are Indian-Chinese style noodles made with unleavened refined wheat flour. These are boiled al dente and then stir fried in a Chinese wok with oil, vegetables & soya sauce




Ingredients:

  • Red Bell Proper
  • Green Bell Proper
  • Yellow Bell Proper
  • Green onion
  • Red onion
  • Ginger Garlic
  • Oil
  • Sauces
  • Noodles

Steps To Cook:

  1. First boil the noodles. Bring 4-5 cups of water to a rolling bowl. Add big pinch of salt and noodles. Let it cook for 4-5 minutes or as per the package instruction. Noodles should be soft, not mushy.
  2. Drain the water. Run under running cold water and then add little oil and toss the noodles. Keep it aside.
  3. Heat the oil in a pan on medium heat. Once hot add chopped ginger, garlic and celery.
  4. Stir and saute for 30-40 seconds. DO NOT brown the garlic.
  5. Now add sliced onions and mix.
  6. Cook till onions get soft and translucent.
  7. Then add all the veggies (carrot, capsicum, cabbage, mushroom)
  8. Mix well and continue cooking.
  9. Cook for 2-3 minutes only. Veggies are just soft, do not overcook them
  10. Now add soy sauce
  11. Add ketchup
  12. Also add chilli sauce.
  13. Add boiled noodles and spring onion greens. Toss everything together till noodles is coated with sauce mixture well. Once noodles get heated through, turn off the stove.

Serve your dish!!

Serving Ideas

Remove it to a serving plate and garnish with more spring onion greens.Because of the addition of various sauces, this veg chow mein noodles tastes good on its own and does not require any side dish. But this can be served with any indo chinese gravy e.g. Gobi manchurian gravy, veg manchurian or mushroom manchurian.
