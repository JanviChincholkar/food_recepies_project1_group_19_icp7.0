Veg-manchurian

About

Through this, you will enjoy not only its delicious taste but also its health benefits. Here are some healthy Manchurian recipe benefits- It has many carbohydrates that can provide instant energy to the body.




Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Cabbage chopped
  • ½ cup Carrots chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ teaspoon Black pepper powder
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 tablespoons Corn flour
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps To Cook:

  1. To make balls, chop all the vegetables finely using knife or use food processor or chopper. Take vegetables (carrot, cabbage, green onion, capsicum) into food processor jar.
  2. Pulse it few times or till they are finely chopped or minced.
  3. Remove it to a bowl. Add salt and peper
  4. Also add all purpose flour and corn flour.
  5. Mix well and try to pinch together using your hand. It should come together and you will be able to form a ball. If it is crumbly and dry then you can sprinkle little water and try binding together. Or if it is too sticky then add more flour. Start shaping all the balls, you will get around 10 balls..

    6. Now Fry It

  6. Now heat the oil in a pan on medium heat for deep frying. Once hot add balls into the oil
  7. Keep moving and turning for even browning.
  8. Once they are fried and golden browned from all the sides, remove them using slotted spatula.And put them into paper towel lined plate. Fry the second batch similarly.
  9. Now to prepare the corn flour mixture, take corn flour in a small bowl. Add 2 tablespoons of water and make lump free mixture. Keep it aside.
  10. Now heat the sesame oil in a pan on medium heat. Once hot add finely chopped ginger, garlic and green chilies.
  11. Saute them for 1 minute, do not brown the garlic.
  12. Now add chopped onions.Mix and continue cooking.
  13. Cook till they are soft and translucent or light pink. Again do not brown the onions, just soften them.
  14. Now add ketchup.
  15. Mix and let it simmer for 2 minutes or till you get nice thick of sauce. Make sure it is not watery. Now add salt and pepper to taste. You can add red chilli flakes too. Mix well.
  16. Now add fried manchurian balls
  17. Toss them and coat all the balls with that sauce.
  18. Lastly sprinkle spring onions greens and serve immediately.

Serve your dish!!

Serving Ideas

While serving the indian chinese meal, serve this veg manchurian dry as a starter dish. Many prefers to have it as a side dish with hakka noodles or Schezwan noodles
