Schezwan Noodles

About

It is a popular flavored noodles recipe made out of the super spicy Schezwan sauce or chutney. Typically a noodles recipe is made with just soy sauce, vinegar, and tomato sauce.




Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • oil
  • ¼ cup Red onion
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • 1 tablespoon Celery chopped finely
  • 2 tablespoons Green beans chopped finely

Steps To Cook:

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil. Then add salt and noodles. Cook as per the package instructions
  2. Do stir with a fork once or twice while boiling to keep the noodles separate
  3. Once it’s done, drain and quickly run it under cold water to stop the cooking process. Add a teaspoon of sesame oil and toss the noodles to coat the oil evenly.
  4. Now heat the oil in a saucepan on medium heat. Once hot add chopped ginger, garlic, celery and onion.
  5. Mix well and saute till onions become soft and translucent.
  6. Heat the oil in a pan on medium-high heat. Once hot add onion, ginger and garlic.
  7. Saute for a minute or two or until the raw smell of ginger-garlic goes away and onion starts to soften.
  8. Then add all the veggies (carrot, beans, peppers, cabbage).
  9. Stir fry for about 4-5 minutes or until they are crisp-tender (meaning veggies have softened slightly yet it has some crunch and freshness. But not mushy or completely cooked.)
  10. Now add all the sauces (schezwan sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, ketchup) and salt.
  11. Mix really well.
  12. Add cooked noodles. Toss noodles and veggies together for a couple of minutes until everything is mixed properly.

Serve your dish!!

Serving Ideas

If you have salted the water beforehand then it takes longer to come to a boil. So always salt the water once it is boiling.
