Veg Cutlet

About

Vegetable Cutlet are crisp, savory, tender patties made with a mix of hearty veggies, savory spices, fresh herbs and crispy breadcrumbs. Cutlet is a popular snack in Indian cuisine.


Ingredients:

  • ½ cup cup chopped carrot
  • ¼ cup chopped french beans
  • 1 cup chopped potatoes
  • ½ cup fresh green peas
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 1 green chili
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • ¼ teaspoon red chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon cumin powder
  • ½ teaspoon Coriander Powder
  • ½ teaspoon Garam Masala
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander
  • salt as required
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 3 to 4 tablespoons water
  • ⅓ cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 to 3 tablespoons oil

Steps To Cook:

    Instructions

    Preparation

  1. Rinse, peel and chop the veggies like carrots, potatoes, french beans.
    2. Boil or steam the veggies until fork tender in a steamer or pressure cooker.
    3. Drain all the water from the cooked veggies very well using a strainer. Set the cooked vegetables aside to become warm.
    4. When the veggies are cooling, blitz 4 to 5 slices of bread in a food processor or blender till fine to get bread crumbs.
    5. Set aside the bread crumbs in a plate or tray.

    Making Cutlet Mixture


    6. When warm, transfer the cooked vegetables in a bowl.
    7. Mash them with a potato masher. Do not make a fine paste but keep it slightly chunkier with tiny bits of veggies. Set aside.
    8. Crush roughly chopped ginger, green chillies and garlic (optional) in a mortar to a semi-fine paste.
    9. Add the crushed ginger+green chili paste, red chili powder, cumin powder, garam masala powder, coriander powder, chopped coriander leaves, bread crumbs and salt as required to the mashed vegetables.
    10.Instead of bread crumbs you can also add 2 to 3 slices of bread dipped in water and then squeezed completely of the water.
    11. Mix everything very well. Check the taste and add more salt or the ground spice powders if needed to the mix vegetable cutlet dough.
    12. In another small bowl, take all-purpose flour and water.
    13. Stir and mix this mixture to get a smooth paste or slurry without any lumps.
    14. Now take the cutlet mixture in a plate and using a cutlet mould, shape it or make small to medium-sized round cutlets by rolling and flattening the mixture in your palms.

    Breading

    15. Take a cutlet and dip in the all-purpose paste gently so that the paste gets coated all over the cutlet.
    16. Then dredge the cutlet in breadcrumbs evenly and all over. Dust off any extra breadcrumbs. Crumb coat and bread each cutlet this way and set aside.
    17. If you do not have breadcrumbs, then you can also use rava (sooji or cream of wheat).

    Pan Frying Veg Cutlet

    18. Heat 2 to 3 tablespoons oil for shallow frying in a skillet or frying pan. Dust the excess bread crumbs from the veggie cutlets sand place them in the medium hot oil.
    19. When one side is light golden or golden, flip and fry the other side. Add more oil if needed.
    20. Flip a couple of time more and fry all the vegetable cutlet till they are evenly golden and crisp from all sides.
    21. Drain them on kitchen paper towels to remove excess oil. Fry the remaining cutlets this way add add more oil if required.
    22. Serve the cutlet hot or warm with mint chutney or coriander chutney or tamarind chutney or tomato ketchup. You can also make burgers, wraps or sandwiches with them.
